Safarka Raysal Wasaaraha Dalka.

Raysal wasaraha dalka ayaa maanta oo Arbaco ah u ambabaxay shirwaynaha 179 ee aad Qaramada midoobay kaasi oo lagaga hadli doono isbadalka dhaqaale iyo amni ee dunida kusoo kordhay gaar ahaa sidii looga hortagi lahaa dagaalka uu ruushku ku qadayo wadamo ka tirsan aasia si uu u soo celiyo midawgii soofiyeeti.

muddada uu joogo raysalwaasaraha dalku shirka qaramada midoobay wuxuu halkaa kulamo gaar gaar ah kula qaadan doonaa madaxyo waynta dawladaha dunida gaar ahaan madaxwaynaha maraykanka
